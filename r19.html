Rasgulla 🍬


Ingredients for rasgulla

  • 1 liter Full Cream Milk 🥛
  • 1 tbsp Lemon Juice 🍋
  • 1 tbsp Vinegar 🍇
  • 2 cups Sugar 🍬
  • 4 cups Water 💧
  • 1/2 tsp Cardamom Powder 🌿
  • 1 tsp Rose Water (optional) 🌹
  • 1 tbsp Cornflour (optional, for binding) 🌽

Steps 🍴

  1. Boil the milk in a pan and add lemon juice or vinegar to curdle it. 🍋🍇
  2. Strain the curdled milk through a muslin cloth to separate the chenna (cottage cheese) from the whey. 🧀
  3. Knead the chenna until smooth and soft, adding a little cornflour if needed. 🌽
  4. Form small balls of the chenna mixture, ensuring they are smooth and round. 🍮
  5. In a large pan, mix sugar and water and bring it to a boil to make a sugar syrup. 🍬💧
  6. Add the chenna balls to the boiling syrup and cook on medium heat for about 20 minutes until they expand and become spongy. 🍯
  7. Once cooked, add cardamom powder and rose water for aroma and flavor (optional). 🌿🌹
  8. Allow the Rasgullas to cool in the syrup before serving. 🍮
