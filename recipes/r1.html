Goan Fish Curry 🍛🐟


Ingredients 📝

  • 500g Fish Fillets (preferably Kingfish or any white fish) 🐟
  • 1 Onion (finely chopped) 🧅
  • 2 Tomatoes (pureed) 🍅
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste 🧄
  • 1 tbsp Red Chili Powder 🌶️
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder ✨
  • 1 tsp Coriander Powder 🌿
  • 1 tbsp Tamarind Paste 🍋
  • 1 cup Coconut Milk 🥥
  • Fresh Cilantro for garnish 🌿
  • Salt and Oil for cooking 🧂🍳

Steps 🍴

  1. Heat oil in a pan and sauté onions until golden brown. 🍳
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 2 minutes. 🧄
  3. Add pureed tomatoes, red chili powder, turmeric, and coriander powder. Cook for 5 minutes. 🍅🌶️✨
  4. Add tamarind paste and a little water to create the curry base. Stir well. 🍋
  5. Now, add the fish fillets and cook until they are tender and fully cooked. 🐟
  6. Pour in coconut milk and simmer for 10 minutes, allowing the flavors to blend. 🥥
  7. Garnish with fresh cilantro before serving. 🌿
