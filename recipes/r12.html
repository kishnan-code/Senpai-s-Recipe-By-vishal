Dhokla 🟨


Ingredients for Dhokla 🟨

  • 1 cup Chickpea Flour (Besan) 🌾
  • 1/4 cup Rice Flour 🌾
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder ✨
  • 1 tsp Eno Fruit Salt (or Baking Soda) 🥄
  • 1/2 tsp Ginger-Green Chili Paste 🧄🌶️
  • 1 tbsp Sugar 🍯
  • 1/2 tsp Salt 🧂
  • 1 cup Water 💧
  • 1 tbsp Lemon Juice 🍋
  • 1 tbsp Oil 🧈

    • For the Tempering:

  • 1 tbsp Oil 🧈
  • 1/2 tsp Mustard Seeds 🌱
  • 1/2 tsp Cumin Seeds 🌱
  • 8-10 Curry Leaves 🌿
  • 1-2 Green Chilies (slit) 🌶️
  • 1 tsp Sugar 🍯 (optional, for sweetness)
  • 1 tbsp Water 💧

    • For Garnish:

  • Fresh Coriander (chopped) 🌿
  • Grated Coconut (optional) 🥥

Steps 🍴

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine chickpea flour, rice flour, turmeric powder, ginger-green chili paste, sugar, and salt. Mix well. 🌾✨🌶️
  2. Gradually add water to the mixture, stirring continuously to form a smooth batter. The consistency should be like a pancake batter. 💧🥄
  3. Add lemon juice and mix again. Then, add **Eno Fruit Salt** (or baking soda) to the batter and gently stir to incorporate. This helps the batter rise and become fluffy while steaming. 🥄🍋
  4. Grease a round steaming dish or a cake tin with a little oil. Pour the batter into the dish, leaving some space at the top as the batter will rise while steaming. 🧈
  5. Prepare your steamer or a large pot with boiling water. Place the steaming dish into the steamer and cover. Steam for 15-20 minutes on medium heat, or until a toothpick inserted into the center comes out clean. 💧🍲
  6. Once the dhokla is steamed, let it cool for a few minutes, then cut it into squares or diamonds. 🍰

Tempering 🍳

  1. In a small pan, heat oil and add mustard seeds. Let them splutter. 🌱🍳
  2. Add cumin seeds, curry leaves, and slit green chilies. Sauté for a few seconds. 🌱🌶️
  3. Add sugar and water to the pan and cook for 1-2 minutes. Pour this tempering over the steamed dhokla. 🍯💧

Garnishing and Serving 🌿

  1. Garnish the dhokla with fresh chopped coriander and grated coconut (optional). 🌿🥥
  2. Serve the dhokla warm with green chutney or tamarind chutney. 🍯🌿
