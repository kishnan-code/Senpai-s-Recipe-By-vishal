Pav Bhaji 🍞🌶️


Ingredients for Pav Bhaji 🍞🌶️

For the Bhaji (Vegetable Mash) 🌶️

  • 2 Large Potatoes (boiled and mashed) 🥔
  • 1 Cup Cauliflower (chopped) 🌸
  • 1/2 Cup Green Beans (chopped) 🥒
  • 1/2 Cup Carrots (chopped) 🥕
  • 1 Onion (finely chopped) 🧅
  • 1 Tomato (chopped) 🍅
  • 1 Bell Pepper (finely chopped) 🌶️
  • 2-3 Green Chilies (slit) 🌶️
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste 🧄
  • 2 tbsp Pav Bhaji Masala (or a mix of garam masala and chili powder) 🌶️✨
  • 1 tbsp Red Chili Powder 🌶️
  • 1/2 tsp Turmeric Powder ✨
  • 1 tsp Cumin Seeds 🌱
  • 2 tbsp Butter (for the bhaji) 🧈
  • Salt to taste 🧂
  • Fresh Cilantro for garnish 🌿
  • Water as needed 💧

For the Pav (Bread Rolls) 🍞

  • 8 Pav (bread rolls) 🍞
  • 2-3 tbsp Butter for toasting 🧈
  • 1/2 tsp Pav Bhaji Masala for sprinkling 🌶️✨

Steps 🍴

Preparing the Bhaji 🌶️

  1. Heat 2 tbsp of butter in a large pan. Add cumin seeds and let them splutter. 🌱
  2. Add chopped onions and sauté until they turn golden brown. 🧅
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste and sauté for 2 minutes until fragrant. 🧄
  4. Add chopped tomatoes, bell pepper, and green chilies. Cook until the tomatoes soften and oil starts separating. 🍅🌶️
  5. Add red chili powder, turmeric powder, and pav bhaji masala. Stir well and cook the spices for 2 minutes. 🌶️✨
  6. Add the chopped vegetables (cauliflower, green beans, carrots) and mix well. Stir for 5 minutes. 🥕🥒🌸
  7. Add the boiled and mashed potatoes to the pan and mix everything together. 🌿
  8. Add a little water to achieve a mash-like consistency. Let it cook on medium heat for 10 minutes, stirring occasionally. 💧
  9. Taste and adjust salt and spices as needed. Garnish with fresh cilantro before serving. 🌿

Preparing the Pav 🍞

  1. Heat a tawa (griddle) or a large pan. Slice the pav in half horizontally. 🥖
  2. Add a little butter to the pan and toast the pav on both sides until golden and crispy. 🧈
  3. Sprinkle a pinch of pav bhaji masala on top of the toasted pav. 🌶️✨

Serving Pav Bhaji 🍴

  1. Serve the hot bhaji in a bowl, garnished with cilantro, and a squeeze of lemon juice. 🍋🌿
  2. Serve with the buttered and toasted pav on the side. Enjoy the Pav Bhaji with some chopped onions and a slice of lemon! 🍋🧅
