Ghevar


Ingredients for Ghevar

  • 1 cup All-Purpose Flour (Maida) 🌾
  • 1/4 cup Semolina (Sooji) 🍚
  • 1/4 tsp Baking Powder ✨
  • 1/2 cup Ghee (clarified butter) 🧈
  • 1/4 cup Cold Water 💧 (adjust as needed)
  • 1/4 cup Milk 🥛
  • A pinch of Saffron strands (optional) 🌼
  • Ghee or Oil for frying 🧈🍳
  • 1 1/2 cups Sugar 🍯
  • 3/4 cup Water (for syrup) 💧
  • 1/4 tsp Cardamom Powder 🌿 (optional)
  • Chopped Pistachios and Almonds for garnish 🌰

Steps 🍴

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine **all-purpose flour**, **semolina**, and **baking powder**. Mix well to ensure even distribution. 🌾🍚✨
  2. Heat **ghee** in a pan. Once hot, pour the ghee into the flour mixture and mix to form a crumbly texture. 🧈🌾
  3. Gradually add **milk** and **cold water** to the mixture and whisk it to make a smooth batter. The batter should be slightly thick but pourable. 🌾💧🥛
  4. If using, soak the **saffron strands** in a tablespoon of warm milk and add it to the batter for flavor and color. 🌼🥛
  5. Heat **ghee or oil** in a deep pan or a vessel to fry the ghevar. The oil should be deep enough to immerse the ghevar. Heat it on medium-high. 🧈🍳
  6. Once the oil is hot, pour a small amount of the batter (around 1-2 tablespoons) into the hot ghee. It will form a ring shape in the oil. You can pour the batter in a thin stream to create the traditional honeycomb-like structure. 🍯🍳
  7. Allow the batter to fry until it becomes golden brown and crispy. Use a slotted spoon to lift the ghevar out of the oil and drain excess oil on a paper towel. 🍯🍳
  1. In a separate pan, add **sugar** and **water** to make the syrup. Bring it to a boil and cook for 5-7 minutes until it thickens slightly. 🍯💧
  2. If desired, add **cardamom powder** and a few saffron strands to the syrup for extra flavor. 🌿🌼
  3. The syrup should be slightly sticky but not too thick (one-thread consistency). Test the consistency by dropping a small amount of syrup into cold water; it should form a soft ball. 🍯
  1. Once the ghevar is fried and crispy, dip it immediately into the warm sugar syrup. Let it soak for about 30 seconds, ensuring the ghevar absorbs the syrup and becomes sweet and sticky. 🍯🍳
  2. After soaking, carefully remove the ghevar and let any excess syrup drip off. Place it on a serving plate. 🍯🍴
  3. Garnish the ghevar with **chopped pistachios** and **almonds** for a crunchy texture and a touch of elegance. 🌰🍯
