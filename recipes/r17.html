Jalebi


Ingredients for Jalebi

  • 1 cup All-Purpose Flour (Maida) 🌾
  • 1/4 cup Rice Flour 🍚
  • 1/4 tsp Baking Powder ✨
  • 1/4 tsp Turmeric Powder (for color) 🌿
  • 1/2 cup Water 💧 (adjust as needed)
  • Ghee or Oil for frying 🧈🍳
  • 1 1/2 cups Sugar 🍯
  • 1/2 cup Water (for syrup) 💧
  • A few Saffron Strands (optional) 🌼
  • 1/4 tsp Cardamom Powder 🌿 (optional)

Steps 🍴

  1. In a mixing bowl, combine **all-purpose flour**, **rice flour**, **baking powder**, and **turmeric powder**. Add water gradually and mix until you get a smooth batter. The batter should be thick but pourable. Let it rest for 30 minutes. 🌾💧
  2. In a pan, add **sugar** and **water** to make the syrup. Stir and bring it to a boil. Once it starts boiling, reduce the heat and let it simmer for 5-7 minutes until it thickens slightly. Add **saffron strands** and **cardamom powder** for flavor and color (optional). 🍯💧🌿
  3. Heat ghee or oil in a wide pan on medium-high heat for frying. You will need enough oil for deep frying. 🧈🍳
  4. While the oil is heating, transfer the batter to a piping bag or a squeeze bottle. The batter should have a consistency that is thick enough to hold its shape but not too runny. 🍶
  5. Once the oil is hot, start squeezing the batter in the shape of circular spirals or any shape you like into the hot oil. Fry the jalebis until they are crispy and golden brown. 🌾🍩
  6. Once the jalebis are fried, immediately dip them in the hot sugar syrup. Let them soak for about 30 seconds, then remove them and place them on a plate. 🍯🔥
  7. Repeat the process for the remaining batter. Serve the jalebis hot, garnished with additional saffron or cardamom, if desired. 🍩🌼
