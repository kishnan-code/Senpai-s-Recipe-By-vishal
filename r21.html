Chicken Biryani 🍚


Ingredients 📝

  • 1 ½ cups Basmati Rice 🍚
  • 500g Chicken (or any protein of choice) 🍗
  • 1 large Onion (thinly sliced) 🧅
  • 2 Tomatoes (chopped) 🍅
  • 1 tbsp Ginger-Garlic Paste 🧄
  • 1 tbsp Red Chili Powder 🌶️
  • 1 tsp Turmeric Powder ✨
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala 🌿
  • 1 cup Yogurt 🧑‍🍳
  • 2-3 Bay Leaves 🍃
  • 4-5 Green Cardamom Pods 🧑‍🍳
  • 2-3 Cloves 🧄
  • Salt to taste 🧂
  • 3 tbsp Ghee or Oil 🛢️
  • Fresh Cilantro and Mint Leaves for garnish 🌿
  • 2 ½ cups Water 💧

Steps 🍴

  1. Wash and soak the rice for 30 minutes. 🍚
  2. Heat ghee or oil in a pot, add whole spices (bay leaves, cardamom, cloves), and sauté until fragrant. 🍃
  3. Add onions and sauté until golden brown. 🧅
  4. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for 2 minutes. 🧄
  5. Add chopped tomatoes, red chili powder, turmeric powder, garam masala, and cook until tomatoes soften. 🍅🌶️✨
  6. Add the chicken and cook until it's browned and partially cooked. 🍗
  7. Stir in yogurt and cook for a few more minutes, then add salt. 🧑‍🍳🧂
  8. In another pot, bring water to a boil, add the soaked rice, and cook until it's 70% done. 🍚💧
  9. Layer the partially cooked rice over the chicken. Add fresh cilantro and mint leaves. 🌿
  10. Cover tightly and cook on low heat (dum) for 15-20 minutes. 🔥
  11. Serve hot, garnished with cilantro and mint. 🌿
